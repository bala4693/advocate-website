Our Legal Services

We offer reliable and professional legal services in both civil and criminal matters. Every case is handled with confidentiality, dedication, and a client-first approach.

Civil Law Services

Our civil law practice focuses on protecting your rights and resolving disputes efficiently through legal consultation and court representation.

  • Property Disputes: Land, house, partition, and ownership issues.
  • Divorce & Family Issues: Divorce, maintenance, custody, and family disputes.
  • Money Recovery: Recovery suits, legal notices, and cheque bounce cases.
  • Agreement & Contract Cases: Drafting, review, breach of agreements.

Criminal Law Services

We provide strong legal defense and representation in criminal cases at all stages, ensuring fair legal process and protection of client rights.

  • Bail Applications: Anticipatory bail and regular bail matters.
  • Fraud & Cheating: Financial fraud and cheating related cases.
  • IPC Cases: Representation for various IPC offences.
  • Assault & Theft: Criminal complaints and defense services.

  • Relevant documents or notices
